What We Do

Taris is the first mobile content network between media buyers and physical venues, helping marketers connect with consumers through a venue's mobile app.

Platform Highlights:

  • Disruptive targeting technology based on real time consumer actions, opposed to mobile profiling. The most powerful targeting spec in the industry.
  • Actionable content including sponsored notifications and native banner ads on iOS and Android phones.
  • Integrated exchange with support for impression and performance based real time bidding.
  • Simple integration with programmatic access to our Content API. A lite front end is also available for smaller media buyers.

Disruptive Targeting

Taris brings a new level of contextual relevance to mobile advertising. Using a combination of hardware and software algorithms, Taris captures consumer action data within venues such as sports arenas, museums, hotels, airports, shopping complexes, and more. This data is then translated into a powerful targeting spec that lets marketers target their ads based on live, real-time consumer action, opposed to mobile profiling, increasing content interactivity and performance.



Contextual Relevance = Happy Consumers

This is the most powerful targeting technology in mobile.

Live-Action Targeting

Target consumers based on the venue they are currently in and what they are doing in that venue, not mobile profiling.

Endless Permutations

Target using broad categories, specific venue names, sub-locations within venues, or by specifying consumer action. The possibilities are endless.

Enhanced Experience

Timely, well targeted ads enhance consumer experience by complimenting current activities, while boosting brand image.


Actionable Content

Taris serves sponsored notifications and native banner ads on iOS and Android phones. Wearable ad types are coming soon!



E-Commerce

"Add to cart" feature lets consumers enter email to save promotion in advertisement and complete transaction at a later time. Clicking on ad takes consumers to a native page in the respective venue's application with further details on promotion. Ad type geared towards brands marketing tangible products to individuals.

Local

"Get directions" feature links with native mapping application to help consumers find local businesses in/around their area. Clicking on ad takes consumers to a native page in the respective venue's application with further details on the local business, along with pictures and Yelp/Google+ reviews. Ad type geared towards small or medium sized local businesses looking to attract consumers currently within their vicinity.


Integrated Exchange

All content on the Taris platform is purchased through our own integrated exchange. Purchase impressions, or place performance based bids. Taris automatically optimizes to maximize results.
